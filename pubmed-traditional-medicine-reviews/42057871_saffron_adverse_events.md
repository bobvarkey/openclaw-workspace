# Adverse Events of Saffron (Crocus sativus): A Systematic Review

**PMID:** 42057871  
**DOI:** 10.1002/hsr2.72212  
**Journal:** Health Science Reports  
**Published:** 2026-04-30  

## Authors
- Hasheminasab FS, Hajimonfarednejad M, Najibi SM, Hashempur MH — *Shiraz University of Medical Sciences, Iran; Canadian College of Integrative Medicine, Canada*

## Keywords
`Crocus sativus` · `adverse effect` · `adverse event` · `saffron` · `systematic review` · `traditional Persian medicine`

## Abstract
Databases including Web of Science, Scopus, and PubMed Central were searched up to August 2024 using keywords "saffron" or "Crocus sativus" combined with safety/adverse event terms. A total of **102 clinical trials and a single case report** were included. Nearly **78% of trials** evaluating the monopreparation of saffron showed favorable safety profiles. Findings suggest saffron is generally well-tolerated at studied doses, though specific adverse events are documented.
