# Impact of Liuwei Dihuang Pills (Decoction) on Early Diabetic Nephropathy: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis

**PMID:** 42051693  
**DOI:** 10.3389/fmed.2026.1802975  
**Journal:** Frontiers in Medicine  
**Published:** 2026-04-29  

## Authors
- Du H, Ren H, Zhang N, Li Y, Zhang B, Ren T, Wang H — *Tianjin University of Traditional Chinese Medicine*

## Keywords
`early diabetic nephropathy` · `liuwei dihuang decoction` · `liuwei dihuang pills` · `meta` · `systematic review`

## Abstract
Meta-analysis of **11 RCTs (1,007 patients)** comparing Liuwei Dihuang Pills + conventional Western medicine vs. Western medicine alone:

**Primary Outcome (UAER):**
- MD = −37.30, 95% CI: −47.51 to −27.09 (favors combination)

**Conclusion:** Liuwei Dihuang Pills combined with conventional Western medicine may provide additional benefits for early diabetic nephropathy with no significant increase in adverse events. However, overall evidence quality is **low** due to small sample size, methodological weaknesses, and incomplete adverse event reporting.
