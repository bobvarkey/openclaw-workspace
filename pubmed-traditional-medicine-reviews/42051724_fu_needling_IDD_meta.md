## Abstract
A meta-analysis of **32 RCTs (2,778 subjects)** found Fu's subcutaneous needling significantly superior to control group:
- **Clinical total effective rate** (RR = 1.16, 95% CI [1.11, 1.20])

Fu's subcutaneous needling therapy demonstrates **good efficacy and safety** for intervertebral disk displacement. However, included study quality is relatively low; more high-quality multicenter RCTs are warranted.
