Essential Hypertension
(diagnosis of exclusion)
⚠ KEY PITFALLS
• Spironolactone is NOT effective for Liddle — defect is downstream of aldosterone receptor; use amiloride / triamterene
• Stop spironolactone, β-blockers, ACEi/ARB before renin/aldosterone testing (if safe)
• Normal imaging does NOT rule out Liddle Syndrome — it's a clinical + genetic diagnosis
💡 KEY PEARLS
• Liddle = ↓ renin + ↓ aldosterone (both low!)
• Conn's = ↓ renin + ↑↑ aldosterone
• Renovascular = ↑ renin + ↑ aldosterone
• Young + HTN + hypokalemia → check renin/aldosterone to distinguish Liddle vs Conn's
