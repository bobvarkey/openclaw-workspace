# Analysis: Secondary Hypertension Diagnosis — Including Liddle Syndrome

## Topic
Medical diagnosis infographic about **Secondary Hypertension** causes, with special emphasis on **Liddle Syndrome** (Pseudo-hyperaldosteronism) as a rare monogenic cause.

## Data Type
- Structured medical reference with:
  - Categorical classification (renal, endocrine, vascular, sleep, drug-induced, monogenic)
  - Diagnostic algorithm (renin-aldosterone pathway)
  - Comparison table for Liddle vs Conn's
  - Key bullet points for each condition
  - Treatment-specific pearls

## Complexity
High — dense medical content with multiple interconnected categories and a diagnostic decision tree. However, well-structured with clear groupings.

## Tone
Professional, educational, clinical — suitable for medical students, residents, and physicians reviewing for exams or clinical practice.

## Audience
- Medical students preparing for board exams (USMLE, PLAB, etc.)
- Residents and clinicians wanting a quick reference for secondary HTN workup

## Source Language
English
