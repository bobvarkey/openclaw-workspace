# Marp

## What It Is

Marp (Markdown Presentation Ecosystem) is a markdown-based slide deck format. Write slides in markdown, render to HTML, PDF, or PowerPoint.

## Use in LLM Wiki

- Generate presentations directly from wiki content
- Answers can be delivered as slide decks
- Obsidian has a Marp plugin

## Example

```markdown
---
marp: true
theme: default
---

# Slide Title

- Point 1
- Point 2

---

# Slide 2

More content...
```
