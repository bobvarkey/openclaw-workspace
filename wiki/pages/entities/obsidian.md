# Obsidian

## What It Is

A markdown-based note-taking and knowledge management app. Recommended as the frontend for browsing an LLM wiki.

## Key Features

- **Graph view**: See the shape of the wiki — what's connected to what, hubs, orphans
- **Backlinks**: Automatically shows links to the current page
- **Quick switcher**: Fast navigation between pages
- **Plugins**: Dataview, Web Clipper, Marp, and more

## Use in LLM Wiki

- Human browses the wiki in real-time while LLM makes edits
- LLM makes edits based on conversation; human observes in Obsidian
- Graph view shows wiki structure at a glance
