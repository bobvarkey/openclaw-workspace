# Antihypertensives ABCDE — Summary

A: ACE-I/ARB. B: Beta-blocker. C: CCB. D: Diuretic (thiazide/loop/K-sparing). E: other (alpha blockers, central agents). First-line: ACE-I/ARB + CCB or thiazide.
