# Diaphragm Normal Values — Summary

## Key Thresholds
- **Thickness at rest**: >0.14 cm
- **Thickening with inspiration**: >20%

## Formula
Thickening Fraction = (Insp − Rest) / Rest × 100

## Reference
Boon et al., Muscle and Nerve 2013
