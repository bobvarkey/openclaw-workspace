# Word of the Day — Saufzwerg

**Word**: Saufzwerg
**Meaning**: "Drinking dwarf" — a small person who gets drunk quickly due to low alcohol tolerance.
**Origin**: German — *saufen* (to drink) + *Zwerg* (dwarf)

## Summary

A delightfully German compound word for someone who can't hold their drink due to size. German compounds are wonderfully literal and evocative.
