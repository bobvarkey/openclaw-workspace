# AVM Assessment — Summary

Assess: nidus (size/compactness/eloquence), shunting (nidal vs fistulous), perinidal findings (aneurysms, stenoses), venous drainage (deep vs superficial, stenoses), shared venous outflow, effects on brain (edema, hydrocephalus).
