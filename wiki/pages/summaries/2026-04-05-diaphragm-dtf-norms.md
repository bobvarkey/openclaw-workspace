# Diaphragm Thickness and DTF — Summary

## Normal Values
- Thickness: Male 2.0-2.1mm, Female 1.7-1.9mm (slightly lower)
- DTF: Male 30-32%, Female 33-35%

## Key Thresholds
- **DTF <20%**: Diaphragm dysfunction
- **DTF <25-33%**: Predicts weaning failure
- **DTF <20%**: Predicts NIV failure
