# 3D Spin DSA for AVMs — Summary

3D spin DSA: better spatial info, surgical planning, volume estimation. 4D DSA adds temporal resolution (arterial 2.2s, nidal 4.2s, venous 6.2s). Microcatheter angiogram for superselective assessment.
