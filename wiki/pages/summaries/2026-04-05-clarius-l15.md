# Clarius L15 Scanner — Summary

## Device
Wireless handheld linear ultrasound probe (15 MHz) for musculoskeletal imaging.

## Key Features
- Wireless, battery-powered
- L15 = high frequency linear probe
- Presets for MSK, knee, vascular
- Mobile device connectivity
