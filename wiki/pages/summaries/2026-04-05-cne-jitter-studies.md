# CNE Jitter Studies — Interpretation Summary

## Summary

A reference guide for interpreting concentric needle electrode (CNE) jitter studies. Abnormal is defined as mean/median exceeding upper limit OR >10% of individual pairs abnormal. Values in microseconds for 6 muscle/protocol combinations.

## Key Parameters

- **Abnormal threshold**: Mean/median abnormal OR >10% individual pairs abnormal
- **Minimum pairs**: 20 acceptable potential pairs
- **Exclude**: Jitter <5 µs
- **Preferred for jitter >150 µs**: Median over mean
- **Always report**: Percentage of blocking

## Reference Values (µs)

| Muscle | Mean UL | Individual UL |
|--------|:-------:|:------------:|
| Orbicularis oculi (vol) | 31 | 45 |
| Orbicularis oculi (stim) | 27 | 36 |
| Frontalis (vol) | 28 | 38 |
| Frontalis (stim) | 21 | 28 |
| Extensor digitorum (vol) | 30 | 43 |
| Extensor digitorum (stim) | 24 | 35 |
