# Diaphragm Layered Structure — Summary

## Key Finding
Diaphragm appears as 3 layers:
- **Hyperechoic (bright)**: Pleura (superior)
- **Hypoechoic (dark)**: Muscular portion (center)
- **Hyperechoic (bright)**: Peritoneum (inferior)

## Clinical Use
Thickness measurement and thickening fraction assessment for diaphragm function.
