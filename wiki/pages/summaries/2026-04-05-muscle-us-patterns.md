# Abnormal Muscle Ultrasound Patterns — Summary

## Three Patterns

| Pattern | Appearance | Category |
|---------|------------|----------|
| Diffuse white | Uniformly bright | Dystrophic |
| Focally abnormal | Localized bright areas | Inflammatory |
| Moth-eaten | Patchy bright/dark mix | Neurogenic |

## Quick Guide

- **Diffuse white** → Think muscular dystrophy
- **Focally abnormal** → Think inflammatory myopathy
- **Moth-eaten** → Think neurogenic (ALS, radiculopathy)
