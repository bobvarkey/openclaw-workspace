# Status Migrainosus — Summary

## Treatment Steps

1. **IV Fluids** + environment (all patients)
2. **Step 1**: IV prochlorperazine or metoclopramide
3. **Step 2**: IV ketorolac or aspirin (NSAID preferred)
4. **Step 3**: SC sumatriptan 6mg (if appropriate)
5. **Step 4**: IV magnesium sulfate
6. **Step 5**: IV chlorpromazine, valproate, levetiracetam, or DHE

## Key Points
- Stepwise approach based on treatment history
- Watch for extrapyramidal side effects (treat with procyclidine)
- Consider GON+ block for refractory cases
