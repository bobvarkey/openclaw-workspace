# Zone of Apposition — Summary

## Key Structure
The ZOA is the portion of diaphragm in **direct contact with the inner surface of the lower rib cage**.

## Functional Importance
- Creates stable base for efficient breathing and core stability
- Contractile force directed through ZOA for rib cage expansion
- Generates intra-abdominal pressure

## Ultrasound Relevance
- Assessment at midaxillary line, 10th ICS
- ZOA thickness and thickening fraction are key parameters
