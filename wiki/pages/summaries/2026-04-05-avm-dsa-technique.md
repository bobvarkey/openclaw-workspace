# AVM DSA Technique — Summary

USS-guided CFA puncture (4Fr sheath, Terumo wire). No Townes view unless essential. Keep same FOV. 3D if helpful. Magnification: AP 32, Lat 40, Mag 20/20 at 7.5f/s.
