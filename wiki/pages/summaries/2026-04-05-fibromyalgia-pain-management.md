# Fibromyalgia & Chronic Pain Management — Summary

## Key Medications
- **First-line**: Paracetamol 4-6g, Pregabalin 300-600mg, Duloxetine 20-60mg
- **Topicals**: Lidocaine patches, Dicloplast, Ketoplast, Buprenorphine 5-10mcg patch
- **Muscle relaxant**: Cyclobenzaprine 15mg ER (Skelebenz)
- **Advanced**: LDN 0.5mg start → 2-4.5mg; Ketamine KIT protocol (0.5mg/kg x 40min x 5 days)

## SPG Block Protocol
Transnasal, parallel to zygoma, weekly x3 then q6-8wks maintenance.

## Combination Notes
Elderly with sciatica often on ≥2 medications.
