# Vaccines Before mAbs — Summary

Complete vaccinations 4–6 weeks before mAb therapy when possible. Live vaccines (MMR) — caution/avoid. Killed vaccines (pneumococcal, influenza, DPT, IPV, Hepatitis B, Shingrix) — safe/give before.
