# Diaphragmatic Excursion — Summary

## Key Measurements
- **B-mode**: Liver and diaphragm anatomy
- **M-mode**: Diaphragmatic Excursion (DE) measurement
- **Device**: GE Venue

## Normal DE Values
- Quiet breathing: 1.8–3.0 cm
- Deep breathing: 5–7 cm

## Clinical Significance
Reduced DE = diaphragm weakness/paralysis. Paradoxical movement = complete paralysis.
