# Nasopharyngeal Swab — Summary

## Key Technique
Insert **parallel to zygoma**, following **superior border of nasal turbinate** along **floor of nasal cavity** toward nasopharynx.

## Correct Path
1. Parallel to zygoma
2. Follow superior turbinate border
3. Along nasal cavity floor
4. To nasopharynx
