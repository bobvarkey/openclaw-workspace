# Status Migrainosus Treatment Algorithm — Summary

## 3-Step Algorithm

| Step | Setting | Key Treatments |
|------|---------|--------------|
| 1 | ED Initial | IV fluids, prochlorperazine, dexamethasone, ketorolac |
| 2 | ED Second-line | Divalproex, magnesium, ondansetron, O2, nerve block |
| 3 | Inpatient | DHE, ketamine, lidocaine, levetiracetam, SPG block |
