# Digastric Muscle Ultrasound — Summary

## Muscles Visualized
- **m. digastricus** — anterior/posterior bellies, elevates hyoid
- **m. geniohyoideus** — above mylohyoid, C1 innervation
- **m. mylohyoideus** — floor of mouth, V3 innervation

## Clinical Context
Submental/suprahyoid region assessment for hypoglossal nerve function and bulbar assessment in ALS/motor neuron disease.
