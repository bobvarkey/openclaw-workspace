# VOGM & Pediatric AVMs — Summary

VOGM: 1:58,000 live births. NGPAVF: 0.1:100,000. Differential in neonates: VOGM > NGPAVF > Dural AVF > AVM. Falcine size determines NAR. Intrauterine treatment possible.
