# Sartorius and TFL Ultrasound — Summary

## Muscles Visualized
- **TFL (Tensor Fasciae Latae)**: Lateral to ASIS; hip flexion, abduction
- **Sartorius**: Medial to ASIS; hip flexion, abduction, rotation

## Landmark
- **ASIS** (Anterior Superior Iliac Spine) — key bony reference

## Clinical Use
Proximal muscle assessment for hip pathology, neuromuscular disease, and sarcopenia.
