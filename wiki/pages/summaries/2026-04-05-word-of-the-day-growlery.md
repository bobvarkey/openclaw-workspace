# Word of the Day — Growlery

**Word**: Growlery (/ˈɡraʊləri/)
**Meaning**: A place of refuge or sanctuary for when one is out of sorts or in ill humor.
**Origin**: English — from *growl* + *-ery* (place of)

## Summary

A charming English word for a very specific human need: a private nook to retreat to when in a bad mood. From the Victorian tradition of having a "place for growling."
