# Skelebenz — Summary

## Drug
Cyclobenzaprine 15 mg ER capsules by Intas. Once daily in the evening for fibromyalgia.

## Mechanism
Centrally acting muscle relaxant; related to TCAs; suppresses muscle spasm via brainstem.

## Key Warnings
- MAOI contraindicated (within 14 days)
- Arrhythmias, HF, active liver disease
- FDA: suicide ideation warning in young patients

## Side Effects
Drowsiness, dry mouth, constipation.
