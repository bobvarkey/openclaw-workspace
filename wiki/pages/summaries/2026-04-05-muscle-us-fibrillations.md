# Dynamic Muscle Ultrasound — Detecting Fibrillations

## Key Point

Dynamic muscle ultrasound can now detect **fibrillations** — previously only detectable by needle EMG. A significant advancement in non-invasive neuromuscular diagnostics.

## Clinical Significance

- Non-invasive alternative to needle EMG for fibrillation detection
- Better for patients who can't tolerate needles
- Allows broader muscle coverage
- Published in Clinical Neurophysiology (Elsevier)
