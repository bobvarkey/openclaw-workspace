# Osteoporosis Prevention — Summary

## Key Data

Oral estradiol: 0.5–2 mg → 3.8–6.7% BMD increase over 2 years
Transdermal estradiol: 0.025–0.1 mg → 2.37–4.7% BMD increase over 2 years

## Critical Clinical Point

**Peak bone loss = late perimenopause** (1–2 years before FMP to 2 years after). This is the optimal window for estrogen therapy to preserve bone density.

## Key Concept

Osteoporosis prevention with HRT is underutilized. The perimenopausal window is the critical opportunity.
