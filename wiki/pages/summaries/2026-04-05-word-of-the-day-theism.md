# Word of the Day — Theism

**Word**: Theism /ˈθiːɪz(ə)m/
**Meaning**: Belief in the existence of a god or gods, specifically a creator who intervenes in the universe.
**Origin**: Greek *theos* (god)

## Summary

A foundational philosophical/religious term. Key distinction: theism = belief God exists AND intervenes; deism = belief God exists but does NOT intervene. Compare with atheism (disbelief) and agnosticism (unknown/unknowable).
