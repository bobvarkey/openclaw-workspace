# SPG Block — Summary

## Anatomy
SPG in pterygopalatine fossa, posterior to middle turbinate.

## Technique
Transnasal catheter over inferior and middle turbinates to pterygopalatine fossa.

## Indications
Cluster headache, migraine, SUNCT/SUNA, facial pain, status migrainosus.

## Medications
Lidocaine or bupivacaine applied topically.
