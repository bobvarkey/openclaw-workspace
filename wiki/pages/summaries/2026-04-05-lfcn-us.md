# LFCN Ultrasound — Summary

## Key Structures
- **LFCN** (Lateral Femoral Cutaneous Nerve): Lateral thigh sensation
- **Sartorius** (SaM): Medial to LFCN
- **TFLM** (Tensor Fasciae Latae): Lateral to LFCN

## Clinical Context
Meralgia paresthetica = LFCN compression causing lateral thigh burning/numbness.

## Adductor Evaluation
Three adductors (longus, brevis, magnus) + gracilis overlay.
