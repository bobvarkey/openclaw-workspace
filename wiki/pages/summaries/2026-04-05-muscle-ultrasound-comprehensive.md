# Muscle Ultrasound — Comprehensive Summary

## Key Parameters
- **Thickness**: Muscle depth measurement
- **Heckmatt grading**: 1-4 scale for echogenicity
- **Frame rate**: ≥20 fps for fibrillation detection

## Key Findings
- **Fasciculations**: Pathologic = regular frequency, polyphasia, increased amplitude, complex/unstable
- **CFs**: Appear only with voluntary contraction, disappear at rest
- **Attenuation patterns**: Diffuse, focal, moth-eaten

## Diaphragm US
- **Zone of Apposition (ZOA)**: Critical for breathing/posture
- **Thickening fraction >30%** (midaxillary line)
- **Tidal volume**: 2 ± 0.5 cm (midclavicular line)

## Sarcopenia Assessment
- Quadriceps muscle thickness + subcutaneous fat
- Splenomegaly: size >12cm, spleen:kidney >1.25
