# Heckmatt Muscle Echogenicity Grading Scale — Summary

## Overview

Standardized 4-point grading scale for muscle echogenicity on ultrasound (1=Normal, 4=Severe changes with unrecognizable texture and strong attenuation).

## Grades

| Grade | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Normal |
| 2 | Slight hyperechogenicity |
| 3 | Texture disruption, some attenuation |
| 4 | Texture unrecognizable, strong attenuation |

## Clinical Use

Used to assess myopathy, muscular dystrophies, and neurogenic changes. Higher grades indicate more severe muscle pathology.
