# Word of the Day — Lalochezia

**Word**: Lalochezia
**Meaning**: The emotional relief gained from using abusive or profane language.
**Origin**: Greek *lalos* (babble) + *chezia* (defecation) — "shitting words"

## Summary

A deliciously clinical word for the very human act of swearing when frustrated. The Greek etymology is wonderfully visceral — literally "shitting words." The relief comes from cathartic expression of pent-up frustration.
