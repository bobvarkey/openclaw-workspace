# Word of the Day — Alis Grave Nil

**Phrase**: Alis Grave Nil (Latin)
**Meaning**: "Nothing is heavy to those who have wings."
**Literal**: "To those who have wings, nothing is heavy."

## Summary

A Latin philosophical phrase about perspective and freedom. With the right mindset and capacity to rise above, burdens become manageable. Evokes Stoic wisdom.
