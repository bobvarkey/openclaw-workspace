# BUPOXONE Buprenorphine Patch — Summary

## Drug
Buprenorphine transdermal patch 5/10 mcg/hr (Intas/ASTERA).

## Indication
Severe opioid-responsive pain not responding to non-opioid analgesics.

## Dosing
5 mcg/hr start, change every 7 days, max 2 patches, rotate sites q3 weeks.

## Key Warnings
Respiratory depression, hyperalgesia, adrenal insufficiency, CYP3A4 interactions.
