# Diaphragm Probe Placement — Summary

## Technique
- Linear probe at **anterior axillary line**, lower costal region
- Target: **Zone of Apposition (ZOA)**
- Patient supine or semi-recumbent

## Key Steps
1. Place linear transducer over lower costal margin
2. Angle caudocephalad toward diaphragm
3. Identify 3-layer structure (pleura, muscle, peritoneum)
