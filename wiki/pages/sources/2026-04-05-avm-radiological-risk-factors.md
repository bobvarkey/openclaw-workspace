# AVM Radiological Risk Factors for Rupture

## Risk Factors for AVM Rupture

| Risk Factor | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Arterial aneurysm** | Associated aneurysms on feeding arteries |
| **Size of nidus** | Smaller AVMs have higher rupture risk |
| **Deep venous drainage** | Drainage to deep veins increases risk |
| **Venous stenosis** | Outflow obstruction |
| **Venous ectasia** | Dilated draining veins |
| **Deep location** | Basal ganglia, periventricular location |

## Anatomical Relationships

- Circle of Willis anatomy relevant to AVM feeders
- Cortical vasculature and venous drainage patterns
- AVM nidus (purple) with feeding arteries and draining veins
