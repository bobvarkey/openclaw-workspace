# 3D Spin DSA for AVMs

## Advantages of 3D Spin DSA

| Advantage | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Spatial information** | Better 3D spatial data vs 2D |
| **Surgical planning** | Visualizes overlying veins |
| **Volume estimation** | More accurate (2D not calibrated) |
| **6D fusion** | Able to fuse 3Ds from different territories |

## Imaging Protocol Comparison

| Parameter | Awake 3D | GK 3D | 4D |
|-----------|:--------:|:-----:|:---:|
| Flow rate (ml/s) | 2 | 2 | 3 |
| Volume (ml) | 15 | 14 | 18 |
| Rise Time (s) | 0.1 | 0.1 | 0.1 |
| Xray delay (s) | 1 | 2 | 0 |

## 4D DSA
Adds **temporal information** to 3D geometry:
- Temporal resolution of contrast flow
- Reconstructs temporal dynamics
- Threshold-based processing
- Angle A and Angle B projections

## Microcatheter Angiogram

### Indications
- Risk stratification
- Treatment planning
- Superselective angiography for:
  - Fistulous points identification
  - Embolization risk assessment (reflux, flow velocity)

### Protocol
- GA, 6Fr Sheath, Guide catheter (?5Fr)
- Microcatheter (size/length according to vessel)
- Selective microcath runs
- Keep FOV/patient/tube position unchanged
- 3D spin if helpful; raw data useful
