# ISSVA Classification — Vascular Anomalies

## ISSVA Classification Overview

Reference: 20th ISSVA Workshop, Melbourne, April 2014; revised May 2018

## Flow-Based Classification

### Low Flow (Blue bracket)

| Type | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Capillary malformations** | Port-wine stain, telangiectasia |
| **Venous malformations** | Slow-flow, compressible |
| **Lymphatic malformations** | Cystic hygroma, lymphangioma |

### High Flow (Red arrow)

| Type | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Arteriovenous malformations (AVM)** | High-flow, arteriovenous shunting |
| **Arteriovenous fistula (AVF)** | Direct AV communication without nidus |

## ISSVA Principle

> This classification is intended to evolve as our understanding of the biology and genetics of vascular malformations and tumors continues to grow.
