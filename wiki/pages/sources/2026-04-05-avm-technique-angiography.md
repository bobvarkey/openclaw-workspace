# AVM Angiography Technique — Siemens Icono

## Initial Angiogram Protocol

**6-Vessel Angiogram:**
- ECA (External Carotid Arteries) — bilateral
- ICA (Internal Carotid Arteries) — bilateral
- VA (Vertebral Arteries) — 1 or 2
- ?Neck arteries

### Key Assessment Points
- Multiplicity of lesions
- Dural supply
- Recruitment from other territories

### Follow-Up Angiograms
Can be more targeted than initial angiogram

## Key Principle

> "If you think about injecting a vessel, do it!"
