# AVM DSA Technique — Radial & Femoral Approach

## Access Technique

| Approach | Use |
|---------|-----|
| **USS guided right CFA puncture** | Initial angiogram |
| **Radial approach** | For follow-up |

CFA = Common Femoral Artery

## Equipment

| Equipment | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Sheath** | 4Fr |
| **Vertebral catheter** | 4Fr Ber, 5Fr Sim2 |
| **Wire** | Terumo 035 (or Terumo 038, Advantage wire) |

## Procedural Notes

- No Townes view; head straight
- Obliquity only if completely necessary
  - Overlying vein obscuring nidus
  - Necessary for understanding anatomy
- Keep same FOV position, centre of image (easier to "fuse")
- 3D if helpful for understanding

## Contrast Injection

- Full strength contrast
- Vary bolus volume (long 20ml injection) according to needs
- Adjust injection strength according to needs

## Magnification Protocol

| View | Magnification | Frame Rate |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| AP | 32 | 7.5 f/s |
| Lateral | 40 | 7.5 f/s |
| Mag up | 20/20 | 7.5 f/s |

## Wire Specifications (Illustrated)

- Distal tapered segment: 2 cm
- Lengths: 180 cm, 300 cm
- Diameters: 0.014", 0.018"
- Tip angle: 35°
- Nitinol core tip, gold coil, hydrophilic coating
