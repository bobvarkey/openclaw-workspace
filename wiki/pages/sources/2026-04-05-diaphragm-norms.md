# Diaphragm Thickness — Normal Values Clinical Pearl

## Clinical Pearl

### Normal Diaphragm Thickness at Rest (End-Expiration)

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Thickness at rest** | **>0.14 cm** |

### Normal Thickening with Deep Inspiration

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Thickening with deep inspiration** | **>20%** |

## Thickening Fraction Formula

**Thickening Fraction** = (Thickness at end-inspiration − Thickness at end-expiration) / Thickness at end-expiration × 100

### Example Calculation

If:
- Thickness at end-expiration (rest) = 0.30 cm
- Thickness at end-inspiration = 0.70 cm

Then:
Thickening Fraction = (0.70 − 0.30) / 0.30 × 100 = **133%**

## Reference

**Boon et al.**
*Two dimensional ultrasound imaging of the diaphragm: quantitative values in normal subjects*
**Muscle and Nerve** 2013, Jun;47(6):884-9

## Clinical Interpretation

| Finding | Interpretation |
|---------|---------------|
| Thickness <0.14 cm at rest | Abnormal (atrophic) |
| Thickening <20% | Abnormal (weakness/paralysis) |
| Thickness ≥0.14 cm AND Thickening ≥20% | **Normal** |
