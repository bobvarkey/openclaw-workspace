# 6D Imaging for AVMs

## Concept

**6D = 3D (spatial) + 3D (temporal from different angles)**

Combines two 3D rotational DSA acquisitions from different angles to provide comprehensive spatial and temporal information.

## Components

| Component | Information |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Spatial 3D** | Anatomy, nidus geometry |
| **Temporal 3D** | Flow dynamics, timing |
| **Dual angle** | A and B projections |

## Temporal Resolution

| Time Point | Phase |
|-----------|-------|
| 2.2 sec | Arterial phase |
| 4.2 sec | Capillary/nidal phase |
| 6.2 sec | Venous phase |

## Clinical Value

- Complete characterization of AVM angioarchitecture
- Simultaneous spatial and temporal understanding
- Improved treatment planning
- Follow-up assessment
