# Intrauterine Fetal Brain MRI

## Technical Details

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Modality** | Intrauterine MRI (IU MRI) |
| **Gestational age** | 32 weeks |
| **Planes** | Axial and Sagittal views |

## Clinical Use

- Prenatal diagnosis of CNS malformations
- Assessment of fetal brain development
- Evaluation of suspected intracranial pathology
- Planning of perinatal management

## Diagnostic Considerations

- High-resolution fetal brain imaging
- Magnetic field safety considerations
- Timing: typically performed after 18 weeks gestation
