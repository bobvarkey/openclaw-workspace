# Status Migrainosus Treatment Algorithm

## Step 1 — Initial Treatment

- **IVF** (IV fluids) ±
- **Prochlorperazine 10 mg** IV ±
- **Diphenhydramine 25 mg** IV ±
- **Dexamethasone 10 mg** IV ±
- **Ketorolac 30 mg** IV
- **Consider DHE 0.5–1 mg** IV

---

## Step 2 — Second-Line Options (If Poor Response to Step 1)

- **Prochlorperazine 10 mg** IV
- **Acetaminophen 1 g** IV
- **Divalproex 500 mg** IV
- **Magnesium sulfate 1 g** IV
- **Ondansetron 4 mg** IV
- **Nerve block**
- **100% Oxygen** 12–15 L via NRB mask

*May repeat — frequency/duration not well established*

---

## If Significant Aura — Additional Options

- **Divalproex 500 mg–1 g** IV
- **Magnesium sulfate 1–2 g** IV
- **Furosemide 20 mg** IV
- **Ketamine 25 mg** IN or **0.1 mg/kg/h** IV
- **Levetiracetam 500 mg–1.5 g** IV
- **Acetazolamide 500 mg–1 g** PO BID
- **Lamotrigine 100 mg** PO BID (outpatient)

*Rule out stroke if relevant*

---

## Step 3 — Consider Inpatient Admission (If Poor Response to Step 2)

| Drug | Dose | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **DHE** | IV every 8h over 3–6 days | |
| **Prochlorperazine** | 10 mg IV | *may repeat |
| **Ketamine** | IV continuous over 4–6 days (max 1 mg/kg/h) | |
| **Lidocaine** | Bolus then IV continuous (max 2 mg/min) | |
| **Levetiracetam** | 500 mg–1.5 g IV | *may repeat |
| **Lacosamide** | 400 mg load, then 200–400 mg IV daily | |
| **Fosphenytoin** | 20 mg/kg IV | *may repeat |

| **Propofol** | 30 mg loading, then 10 mg boluses | |
| **Suprazygomatic SPG block** | | |
| **Inpatient behavioral treatments** | | |
