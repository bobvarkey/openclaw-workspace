# Theism

**Pronunciation**: /ˈθiːɪz(ə)m/

**Part of Speech**: Noun

**Definition**: Belief in the existence of a god or gods, specifically of a creator who intervenes in the universe.

**Origin**: From Greek *theos* (god)

---

## Usage

- "Theism stands in contrast to atheism — the denial of God's existence."
- "There are many different forms of theism across world religions."

## Related Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Deism** | Belief in God but NOT that God intervenes in the universe |
| **Atheism** | Disbelief in the existence of God/gods |
| **Agnosticism** | Belief that the existence of God is unknown or unknowable |
| **Monotheism** | Belief in one God (e.g., Christianity, Islam, Judaism) |
| **Polytheism** | Belief in multiple gods (e.g., Hinduism) |

## Theism vs Deism

| | Theism | Deism |
|--|--------|-------|
| God exists | Yes | Yes |
| God intervenes | Yes | No |
| Revelation | Accepted | Rejected |
