# Vein of Galen Malformation (VOGM)

## Definition

Congenital AV shunt into the median prosencephalic vein in the velum interpositum of the 3rd ventricle.

## Normal Venous Anatomy

| Structure | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Superior sagittal sinus** | Drains superior cerebral veins |
| **Inferior sagittal sinus** | Drains inferior cerebral veins |
| **Straight sinus** | Formed by great cerebral vein + inferior sagittal sinus |
| **Thalamostriate vein** | Drains corpus striatum |
| **Internal cerebral vein** | Paired, drains deep brain |
| **Basal vein of Rosenthal** | Drains basal brain structures |
| **Vein of Galen** | Formed by union of internal cerebral veins |

## VOGM Anatomy

- Dilated prosencephalic vein ( vein of Galen )
- Enlarged, tortuous arterial feeders (choroidal arteries)
- Shunting directly into the dilated venous pouch

## Classification

- **Choroidal type**: VGAM (Vein of Galen Aneurysmal Malformation) / VGAD
- **Pial type**: pial AVF
