# Alis Grave Nil

**Language**: Latin phrase

**Translation**: "Nothing is heavy to those who have wings." (Literally: "To those who have wings, nothing is heavy.")

---

## Meaning

A philosophical expression about perspective and freedom. When you have the strength, resilience, and freedom to rise above circumstances, no burden feels too heavy.

## Usage

- "When faced with seemingly insurmountable challenges, remember: alis grave nil — nothing is heavy to those with wings."
- "The创业者's motto: alis grave nil."
- "As she took flight into the unknown, she whispered alis grave nil."

## Interpretation

- **Literal**: Something heavy (grave) becomes light (not heavy) when you have wings
- **Metaphorical**: With the right perspective, tools, or freedom, what seems burdensome becomes manageable
- **Stoic undertone**: The weight of life depends on your capacity to transcend it
