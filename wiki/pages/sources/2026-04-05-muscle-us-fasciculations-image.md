## Clinical Context

This ultrasound image demonstrates **fasciculations** — visible involuntary twitches of muscle fibers detected via dynamic muscle ultrasound rather than needle EMG.

### Parameters Used
- **Frame rate**: ≥20 fps for dynamic detection
- **Frequency**: 72 Hz — determines resolution vs penetration
- **Depth scale**: 0–4 cm depth range

## Fasciculations on Dynamic Ultrasound

- Visible as spontaneous twitches within the muscle
- Can be recorded and analyzed without needle insertion
- Frequency, amplitude, and morphology can be assessed
- Useful for ALS and other motor neuron disease diagnosis
