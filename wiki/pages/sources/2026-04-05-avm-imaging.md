# AVM Imaging — Protocols & Techniques

## Presenter
Dr. Anne Schmitt — ECMINT

## Why Image AVMs?

- Diagnosis and confirmation
- Treatment planning
- Risk stratification
- Follow-up assessment

## Imaging Modalities

| Modality | Use |
|---------|-----|
| **CT/CTA** | Initial evaluation, hemorrhage detection, bone anatomy |
| **MRI/MRA** | Soft tissue, brain parenchyma, flow voids, nidus characterization |
| **DSA** | Gold standard; flow dynamics, angioarchitecture, embolization planning |

## Multimodal Imaging Approach

- Initial: CT/CTA for screening
- MRI/MRA for parenchymal assessment
- DSA for definitive diagnosis and treatment planning
