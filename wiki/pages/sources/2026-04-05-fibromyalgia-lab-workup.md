# Fibromyalgia Lab Workup — Extended Panel

## Sleep Assessment

| Test | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Belun** | Home sleep apnea screening device |

## Hormonal Assessment

| Test | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Free testosterone** | May be low in fibromyalgia/FMS |
| **Free T3** | Thyroid function; low T3 syndrome in chronic illness |
| **Holotranscobalamin** (Active B12) | Preferred over standard B12 — reflects functional B12 status |

## Metabolic/Inflammatory Markers

| Test | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Homocysteine** | Methylation, B12/folate status, CV risk |
| **RBC magnesium** | Red cell magnesium — better tissue indicator than serum |
| **hSCRP** | High-sensitivity CRP — inflammation |
| **GGT** | Gamma-glutamyl transferase — liver, oxidative stress |

## Infectious Disease

| Test | Indication |
|------|-----------|
| **Lyme serology** | Rule out Lyme disease if endemic area / unclear etiology |

## Rationale for Extended Workup

Fibromyalgia is a diagnosis of exclusion. These tests help:
1. Rule out treatable conditions mimicking FMS
2. Identify comorbid conditions (sleep apnea, thyroid disease)
3. Guide personalized treatment
4. Optimize targeted therapies

## Clinical Pearls

- **Sleep apnea** commonly co-occurs with fibromyalgia — Belun home study first-line
- **Low testosterone** — both free and total; consider replacement if symptomatic
- **RBC magnesium** > serum magnesium; repletion may help muscle symptoms
- **Holotranscobalamin** — more sensitive marker of B12 deficiency than MMA
