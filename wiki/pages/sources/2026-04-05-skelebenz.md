## Mechanism

Centrally acting skeletal muscle relaxant structurally related to tricyclic antidepressants. Works through the brainstem to suppress muscle spasm without affecting neuromuscular function.

## Clinical Use in Fibromyalgia

- Reduces muscle spasm associated with fibromyalgia
- Once-daily dosing (evenings)
- Extended-release formulation for sustained effect

## Contraindications

- Hypersensitivity to cyclobenzaprine
- **Recent MAOI use (within 14 days)** — risk of serotonin syndrome
- Recovery phase of MI
- **Arrhythmias, HF, conduction disturbances**
- **Angina pectoris**
- Active liver disease

## Pregnancy & FDA Warning

- **Pregnancy**: Category B
- **FDA Tricyclic Antidepressant Warning**: Increased suicide ideation in children, adolescents, and young adults
