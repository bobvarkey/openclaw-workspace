# GAD-7 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7)

## Definition

A 7-item self-administered screening tool for generalized anxiety disorder and other anxiety disorders.

**Reference**: Spitzer RL, et al. Arch Intern Med. 2006

## Scoring

| Score | Severity |
|-------|----------|
| 0–4 | Minimal anxiety |
| 5–9 | Mild |
| 10–14 | Moderate |
| 15–21 | Severe |

## Items Cover
1. Feeling nervous/anxious
2. Not being able to stop worrying
3. Worrying too much about different things
4. Trouble relaxing
5. Being so restless that it's hard to sit still
6. Becoming easily annoyed/irritable
7. Feeling afraid as if something awful might happen

## Clinical Use

- Initial anxiety screening
- Monitoring treatment response
- Often used alongside PHQ-9 for comorbidity assessment
