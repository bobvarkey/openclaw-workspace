# Vocabulary

## Definition

A collection of words — learned, curated, and maintained over time. Vocabulary building is a lifelong process of language acquisition and refinement.

## Building Vocabulary

### Methods
- **Word of the Day**: One new word each day, saved and reviewed
- **Reading**: Exposure to new words in context
- **Etymology**: Understanding word origins makes them easier to remember
- **Active Use**: Using new words in writing and speech

### Word Selection Criteria
- Interesting etymology
- Useful in everyday communication
- Evocative or unusual meaning
- Borrowed from other languages (like *sillage* from French)

## Value of a Personal Vocabulary Wiki

- Words are organized and searchable
- Easy to review and revisit
- Cross-linked with related words and concepts
- Includes personal context (where/when learned, examples)
