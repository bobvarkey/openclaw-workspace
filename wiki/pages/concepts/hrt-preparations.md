# HRT Preparations

## Estrogen Types

### Transdermal Estradiol (Preferred)
| Form | Brand Examples | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Patch | Climara, Vivelle-Dot | Changed 1–2x/week |
| Gel | EstroGel, Divigel | Daily application |
| Spray | Evamist | Daily forearm spray |

**Advantage**: Bypasses hepatic first-pass → no increased VTE risk

### Oral Estrogens
| Form | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Estradiol tablets | Estrace, generic |
| CEE (Conjugated Equine Estrogen) | Premarin (used in WHI) |

**Note**: Oral formulations increase hepatic clotting factor synthesis

---

## Progestogen Types

### Micronized Progesterone (Preferred)
- **Brand**: Prometrium, Crinone (vaginal)
- Natural, body-identical progesterone
- Better tolerated; lower cardiovascular/metabolic risks
- Can cause drowsiness (take at bedtime)

### Synthetic Progestins
| Type | Notes |
|------|-------|
| MPA (medroxyprogesterone acetate) | Used in WHI; higher VTE and breast cancer risk |
| Norethindrone acetate | Androgenic progestin |
| Drospirenone | Anti-androgenic, anti-mineralocorticoid |

---

## Combined Preparations

| Route | Type | Example |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Transdermal** | E2 patch + oral micronized P4 | Standard modern approach |
| **Oral** | E2 + norethindrone | Activella, CombiPatch |
| **Vaginal** | Local E2 only | Vagifem, Estring — for GSM only, minimal systemic absorption |

---

## Key Principle

**Modern HRT = Transdermal E2 + Micronized Progesterone**
- Same efficacy as oral preparations
- No increased VTE risk
- Better tolerated
