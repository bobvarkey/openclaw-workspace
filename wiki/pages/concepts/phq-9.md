# PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire-9)

## Definition

A 9-item self-administered screening tool for depression severity. Based on DSM criteria for major depressive disorder.

## Scoring

| Score | Severity |
|-------|----------|
| 0–4 | None/minimal |
| 5–9 | Mild |
| 10–14 | Moderate |
| 15–19 | Moderately severe |
| 20–27 | Severe |

## Item 9: Self-Harm

⚠️ Item 9 screens for suicidal ideation/self-harm:
- "Thoughts that you would be better off dead or of hurting yourself in some way"
- **Any positive response requires immediate safety assessment**
- Mental health referral is urgent if positive

## Clinical Use

- Initial depression screening
- Monitoring treatment response
- Not a diagnostic tool — positive screen warrants further evaluation

## Limitations

- Self-report — subject to response bias
- Does not distinguish between grief and depression
- Item 9 is the most critical item for safety
