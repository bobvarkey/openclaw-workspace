### 1. Impact of Intravenous Thrombolysis on Aspiration Thrombectomy Outcomes
**Journal:** JNIS (Published May 12, 2026)

**Why it matters clinically:** If tPA + aspiration yields better first-pass reperfusion than aspiration alone, this could change pre-thrombectomy protocols — especially relevant for centers using direct-to-angiography triage.
