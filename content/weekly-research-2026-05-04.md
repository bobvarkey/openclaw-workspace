## 1. Neuralink / BCI Updates

### Neuralink Moving to High-Volume Production
Neuralink announced plans to scale "The Link" brain implant to high-volume production in 2026, with a streamlined, almost entirely automated surgical procedure. Musk stated device threads will go through the dura without needing to remove it — a significant surgical advancement. The PRIME study is actively recruiting at Barrow Neurological Institute (Phoenix) and University of Miami, targeting ~20 patients implanted by late 2025 with plans for dozens more in 2026.

**12-Month Outcomes:** Neuralink reported 12-month outcomes in five participants with tetraplegia, with all demonstrating thought-controlled cursors, typing, and even 3D design capabilities post-surgery.

**→ Content angle:** "Neuralink just dropped 12-month human trial results — here's what actually happened." Good for YouTube deep-dive or X thread.

- [Source: Neuralink PRIME Study (clinicaltrials.gov)](https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT06429735)
- [Source: The Debrief — High-Volume Production](https://thedebrief.org/neuralink-set-to-launch-high-volume-brain-implant-production-as-competitors-weigh-in/)
- [Source: BCI Intel — 12-Month Outcomes](https://bciintel.com/)

### Synchron BCI Progress
Synchron raised $200M in Series D financing to advance its Stentrode endovascular BCI platform toward commercialization. The COMMAND study is ongoing, and their FOCUS-AUS trial (NCT07533903) was updated April 9. Unlike Neuralink's invasive surgery, Synchron's BCI is delivered via blood vessels — no open brain surgery required.

**→ Content angle:** "The BCI you don't need brain surgery for" — great explainer comparing Neuralink vs Synchron vs China approaches.

- [Source: Synchron — Current Trials](https://synchronbci.com/)

### Other BCI Players

- **Neurotech Notables** reports $200M+ deployed to Axoft, Cala, Nervonik, Sinaptica, and others in April 2026 alone.

## 2. AI in Medicine / Neurology News

### Prima AI — Brain MRI Diagnosis in Seconds
University of Michigan's "Prima" AI can read brain MRIs and diagnose neurological conditions in seconds with up to **97.5% accuracy** across 50+ conditions. Flags emergencies like strokes in real time. Published in Nature Neuroscience.

**→ Content angle:** "AI just got better at reading brain scans than most radiologists" — explosive X thread material.

- [Source: SciTechDaily](https://scitechtoday.com/this-ai-diagnoses-brain-disorders-in-seconds/)
- [Source: Michigan Medicine](https://www.michiganmedicine.org/health-lab/ai-model-can-read-and-diagnose-brain-mri-seconds)

### BrainIAC — AI Foundation Model for Brain Scans

- [Source: Psychology Today](https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/blog/the-future-brain/202602/ai-foundation-model-predicts-diseases-from-brain-scans)
