# Pioglitazone for Inclusion Body Myositis

## Overview
Inclusion Body Myositis (IBM) is a progressive inflammatory myopathy characterized by muscle weakness, mitochondrial dysfunction, and metabolic dysregulation.

## Key Study
**Nature Communications, March 2026**
- PMID: 41832158
- Title: "Modifying muscle metabolic dysregulation in inclusion body myositis with pioglitazone: a single-arm trial"

## Trial Details
- **Design**: Single-arm, open-label phase 1 trial
- **Participants**: IBM patients
- **Treatment**: Pioglitazone 45mg daily
- **Duration**: 
  - 16-week observation (lead-in) period
  - 32-week treatment period

## Mechanism
- PPARγ agonist
- Targets muscle metabolic dysregulation
- Improves mitochondrial function

## Clinical Significance
First trial showing pioglitazone (a diabetes drug) can modify IBM pathophysiology by addressing underlying metabolic dysfunction.
