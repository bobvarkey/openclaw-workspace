## Hero Section
**The Problem (felt):**
Every minute in stroke care, you're racing against a clock that should have been your ally.

You're a neurologist or neurointerventionalist. You see the CT, the CTA, the ASPECTS score. And then you have to decide: tPA? Thrombectomy? Transfer? Watch and wait?

The guidelines exist. But they're written for populations, not patients. And the patient in front of you has 4 comorbidities, 2 prior strokes, and a family that's asking you what to do.

Right now, you make that call with your training, your experience, and a lot of mental math under pressure.

**What If You Had a Second Brain?**
NeuroGuide AI ingests your patient's imaging and clinical data — and surfaces exactly what the evidence says about treatment options, timing windows, and risk profiles.

Built by a neurointerventionalist, for neurointerventionalists. Not a black box. Full reasoning, cited guidelines, adjustable for local protocols.

## Features Section

### 1. Stroke Triage Intelligence
- Automated ASPECTS scoring from CT
- LVO detection on CTA
- Treatment recommendation: tPA eligibility, thrombectomy window assessment
- Door-to-needle / door-to-puncture time optimization

**Who it's for:** ED neurologists, stroke neurologists, neurocritical care teams

### 2. Aneurysm Treatment Planner
- Flow diverter vs coiling vs clipping decision support
- Patient-specific risk profiling based on morphology, location, and comorbidities
- Antiplatelet planning for flow diversion
- Long-term follow-up scheduling

**Who it's for:** Neurointerventionalists, neurosurgeons, cerebrovascular fellows

### 3. ICU Neurocritical Care Protocol Assistant
- ICP management decision trees
- Status epilepticus escalation protocols
- Coma prognostication checklist (based on latest 2025-2026 data)
- Continuous EEG monitoring guidance

**Who it's for:** Neurocritical care intensivists, ICU teams, neurology residents

### 4. Evidence Watch
- Real-time literature alerts for your subspecialty
- Automated paper summaries relevant to your patient mix
- Weekly digest: top 5 papers in stroke, neurointervention, neurocritical care

**Who it's for:** All of the above — stay current without reading 40 papers a week

## Objection Handling
*"This is just AI giving me advice I already know"*

Fair. But:
1. It synthesizes 10+ guidelines and hundreds of papers into one view — in seconds
2. It shows its reasoning and citations, so you're not trusting a black box
3. It's built by someone who makes these decisions clinically — not a tech company that read about medicine

*"I'm not giving patient data to the cloud"*

Good instinct. MVP is:
- On-premise deployment option for enterprise
- HIPAA-compliant infrastructure
- No patient data used for model training (opt-in only)
