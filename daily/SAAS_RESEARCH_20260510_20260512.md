## Key Market Gaps

1. No bridge between cognitive training and actual work output
2. Real-time AI personalization still rudimentary  
3. Proactive cognitive decline prevention for healthy adults — wide open
4. Neurodivergent-affirming tools that optimize (not just accommodate)
5. Multi-modal cognitive health (training + sleep + nutrition) — fragmented

---

## 5 SaaS MVP Ideas

### 1. FocusFlow — AI Cognitive Performance Tracker ⭐ FASTEST
- Target: Knowledge workers 25-45
- Core: Real-time Focus Score + neuroscience micro-interventions
- Why: Lightweight consumer tier doesn't exist, $9-15/mo

### 2. CogniAge — Proactive Brain Health
- Target: Adults 40-65
- Core: Monthly cognitive assessments + personalized training
- Why: No proactive prevention SaaS exists

### 3. NeuroScaffold — AI Executive Function Assistant
- Target: ADHD/autistic professionals (15-20% workforce)
- Core: Slack/email integration, cognitive scaffolding
- Why: Corporate DEI budgets funding neuroinclusion

### 4. SynapseLMS — Adaptive Learning Platform
- Target: Corporate L&D teams
- Core: Cognitive profiling + spaced repetition
- Why: LMS market lacks neuroscience delivery

### 5. MindLoad — Cognitive Capacity Forecasting
- Target: Engineering managers, HR
- Core: Calendar/energy analysis, burnout forecasting
- Why: 89% burnout undetected until too late

---

## Recommendations

- **Fastest MVP:** FocusFlow (6-8 weeks)
- **Highest Potential:** CogniAge
- **Most Defensible:** SynapseLMS
