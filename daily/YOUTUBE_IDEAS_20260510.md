## 🎬 Video Idea 1
**Title:** "Your Brain on AI: What Dopamine Fasting Gets Wrong (Neuroscience Explains)"

**Hook/Angle:** Dopamine fasting is trending, but the neuroscience says something different. AI tools are changing your brain's reward circuitry in ways most productivity gurus don't understand. This video debunks popular claims using real fMRI research and explains what actually works.

**Target Keywords:** dopamine fasting, neuroscience productivity, AI brain effects, dopamine detox, focus neuroscience, how AI changes your brain

**Why it would perform well:**
- Dopamine content is HUGE — Huberman's "Controlling Your Dopamine" has **12.2M views**, and CNN just ran a dopamine piece (Apr 30, 2026). This is peak cultural timing.
- "AI is messing with your brain" taps into anxiety + curiosity — high CTR combination.
- The "debunking" format (mistake/truth framework) earns trust and drives comments/engagement.
- Combines two massive trending topics (neuroscience + AI) into one unique angle.
- YouTube's 2026 algorithms favor deep-dives over generic explainers — this earns its runtime.
