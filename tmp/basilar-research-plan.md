# Research Plan: Basilar Thrombectomy — Neurointervention Perspective

## Themes (4)

### Theme 1: Historical Evolution of BAO Management
- Natural history of basilar occlusion (pre-EVT era)
- Early endovascular experience (IA thrombolysis, first-generation devices)
- Shift to modern EVT: stent retrievers, aspiration

### Theme 2: Pivotal Clinical Trials
- MR CLEAN (anterior) → extrapolation to posterior circulation
- ATTENTION trial (2022-2023)
- BAOCHE trial (2022-2023)
- BASICS trial
- POST-ATTENTION / POST-BAOCHE analyses
- Ongoing trials (DISTAL, etc.)

### Theme 3: Technical & Device Considerations
- Stent retrievers vs aspiration in basilar
- Access challenges (vertebral tortuosity, subclavian)
- Balloon guide catheter role in posterior circulation
- Anesthesia considerations
- Imaging selection (pc-ASPECTS, posterior circulation collateral scores)

### Theme 4: Outcomes, Complications & Knowledge Gaps
- Mortality and functional outcomes
- Hemorrhagic transformation risk in posterior circulation
- Time window expansion evidence
- Patient selection challenges
- Current controversies + future directions

## Approach
- Two cycles of research per theme
- Web search → web fetch primary sources → analyze
- Cross-theme integration at the end
- Full APA 7 citations
