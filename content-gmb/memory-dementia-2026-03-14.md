🧠 Understanding Memory & Dementia

Memory changes are a normal part of aging, but when should you be concerned? Dementia involves more than just forgetfulness—it affects daily functioning, reasoning, and communication.

Early signs include:
• Difficulty finding words
• Getting lost in familiar places
• Changes in mood or personality
• Trouble managing finances

Early diagnosis matters. With proper evaluation, some causes of memory loss are treatable, and early intervention can slow progression in dementia.
