## 3. YouTube Growth Tactics (2026)

### What's Working
- **Shorts first, long video recaps** — post 60-90s shorts, drive to 10-15 min video
- **Controversial/debunking titles** — "You're Wrong About Sleep" performs well
- **Personal story + science** — "What happened to my brain when I did 30 days of..."
- **Thumbnail formula:** Bold face + contrasting color text + emotion (fear or curiosity)
- **First 30 seconds are everything** — Hook with a stat or a question, never a generic "today we'll talk about"

### Topic Clusters That Rank
1. Sleep + Alzheimer's prevention
2. Neuroplasticity + recovery
3. Nootropics + evidence
4. Cognitive enhancement for entrepreneurs
