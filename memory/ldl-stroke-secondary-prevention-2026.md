# LDL Targets After Stroke — Research Brief
Date: 2026-04-04

Status: COMPLETE

## Key Findings
1. LDL-C goal <55 mg/dL (1.4 mmol/L) for very high-risk ASCVD including ischemic stroke
2. Consider <40 mg/dL for recurrent events or polyvascular disease
3. Non-HDL-C <85 mg/dL as coprimary target
4. Combination therapy (statin + ezetimibe ± bempedoic acid ± PCSK9i) needed to reach goals
5. Lp(a) measurement now recommended universally
6. PREVENT-ASCVD equations replacing Pooled Cohort Equations for 10-30 year risk
