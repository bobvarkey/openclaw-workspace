## 1. Neurointervention / Stroke / Thrombectomy

### High-Impact Papers (Today)

**Laterality Matters in MCA Stroke**  
Albert et al. *Stroke* (Mar 13, 2026) — PMID 41822970  
Right MCA strokes were significantly more likely to receive EVT (aOR 1.26, p<0.001). Left MCAs got more IV thrombolysis. Raises questions about treatment bias and outcome asymmetry.  
*Content angle: "Does stroke side determine your treatment?"*

**NLR-FAR Index Predicts Post-EVT Outcomes**  
Meng et al. *Front Neurol* (Feb 25, 2026) — PMID 41821631  
Neutrophil-to-lymphocyte ratio + fibrinogen-to-albumin ratio combo index predicts 30-day functional outcome after thrombectomy. Simple blood test, powerful prognostic signal.  
*Content angle: "A blood test that predicts your stroke recovery"*

**Systemic Immune-Inflammation Index Post-MT**  
Zhou et al. *Front Neurol* (Feb 25, 2026) — PMID 41821630  
SII as superior predictor of functional outcome after mechanical thrombectomy. Median age 68. Inflammation drives secondary brain injury post-reperfusion.  
*Content angle: "Why inflammation, not just recanalization, decides stroke outcomes"*

**Reperfusion Outcomes by NIHSS-ASPECTS Mismatch**  
Schlicht et al. *Ann Clin Transl Neurol* (Mar 12, 2026) — PMID 41820257  
German Stroke Registry data. Clinical-core mismatch stratifies who benefits most from reperfusion.  
*Content angle: "Who really benefits from thrombectomy? The mismatch theory"*

**Transradial CAS with 8-Fr Balloon Guide Catheter**  
Kojima et al. *J Neuroendovasc Ther* (Mar 10, 2026) — PMID 41821642  
Feasibility/safety series of transradial carotid stenting with large-bore BGCs. Expanding radial access in neurointervention.  
*Content angle: "Wrist access for brain stenting — the radial revolution continues"*

### Emerging Trend: Neurovascular Coupling (NVC)
Multiple papers (van Dijk et al., Fesharaki et al.) highlight NVC as early biomarker for cognitive decline and aging. Functional marker, not structural.  
*Content angle: "Your brain's blood flow response — the earliest sign of dementia"*

## 2. Epilepsy

**LGI1-Antibody Encephalitis: Localizing Faciobrachial Dystonic Seizures**  
Ahn et al. *Epilepsia* (Mar 13, 2026) — PMID 41823021  
Neuroanatomical localization of the signature seizure semiology. Key for early diagnosis before antibodies return.

**Thalamic RNS After Hemispherectomy**  
Stanton et al. *J Child Neurol* (Mar 13, 2026) — PMID 41823012  
Responsive neurostimulation of thalamus as option for drug-resistant epilepsy after anatomic hemispherectomy. Novel surgical-neuromodulation combo.

**Carbon Dots for Neuroprotection in Epilepsy**  
Cheng et al. *Front Mol Biosci* (Feb 25, 2026) — PMID 41822390  
Traditional Chinese medicine-derived carbon dots with multi-target antiepileptic effects in mouse models. Three chemoconvulsant models tested.  
*Content angle: "Ancient medicine meets nanotech for epilepsy"*

## 3. Dementia / Alzheimer's Disease

**Shared Genetics: AD + Psychiatric Disorders**  
Kumar et al. *Alzheimers Dement* (Mar 2026) — PMID 41823034  
TMEM106B, ACE, and ERC2 identified as shared loci between Alzheimer's and primary psychiatric disorders. Genetic overlap may explain neuropsychiatric symptoms in AD.

**Hypertension as Major AD Risk Factor**  
Jiang et al. *Clin Interv Aging* (Mar 7, 2026) — PMID 41822915  
Review: midlife HTN predicts late-life dementia via endothelial dysfunction, BBB disruption, white matter disease. Therapeutic targets discussed.

**Lipid Accumulation Product Index + Dementia Risk**  
Liu et al. *J Alzheimers Dis* (Mar 13, 2026) — PMID 41823033  
Sex-specific abdominal lipid marker (LAP) associated with dementia in rural elderly. Population-based.

**MCI to Dementia Progression: Social + CV Risk Factors**  
Miramontes et al. *medRxiv* (Mar 3, 2026) — PMID 41822695  
EHR-based study. Social isolation + cardiovascular risks as predictors of MCI conversion. Preprint.

**Immunotherapies for Neurodegenerative Diseases: Sex Differences**  
Cortes-Flores & Brinton. *medRxiv* (Mar 6, 2026) — PMID 41822688  
Preprint. Immunotherapy risk reduction in AD, PD, MS, ALS — sex and treatment duration matter. Brinton lab continues to lead on sex-based neurodegeneration.

## 4. Neuromuscular / ALS

**Plasma isomiRs as ALS Biomarkers**  
Magee et al. *Neurol Genet* (Mar 10, 2026) — PMID 41822037  
MicroRNA isoforms (isomiRs) in plasma outperform canonical miRNAs as ALS biomarkers. Liquid biopsy angle.

**Tofersen in SOD1 p.Leu145Phe ALS: Croatian Real-World Data**  
Bilić et al. *Amyotroph Lat Scler Frontotemporal Degener* (Mar 13, 2026) — PMID 41821425  
Real-world outcomes of intrathecal tofersen in genetically homogeneous cohort. SOD1 mutation-specific therapy showing promise.

**POLG Mitochondrial Disease: Clinical Heterogeneity**  
Bermejo-Guerrero et al. *Neurol Genet* (Mar 10, 2026) — PMID 41822038  
Candidate biomarkers for POLG-related disease. Spectrum ranges from epilepsy to myopathy.

**Dysphagia in Myositis/Muscular Dystrophy: Real-Time MRI + Quantitative Ultrasound**  
Zeng et al. *J Cachexia Sarcopenia Muscle* (Apr 2026) — PMID 41821399  
Novel imaging combo for swallowing dysfunction in inflammatory myopathies and dystrophies.
