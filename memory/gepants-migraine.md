# Migraine Prevention - Gepants Update

Date added: 2026-03-25

## Gepants - CGRP Receptor Antagonists

Gepants are small molecule CGRP receptor antagonists for acute and preventive migraine treatment.

### Available Gepants

| Drug | Brand | Dose | Use |
|------|-------|------|-----|
| Ubrogepant | Ubrelvy | 50-100mg | Acute |
| Rimegepant | Nurtec ODT | 75mg | Acute + Preventive (QOD) |
| Atogepant | Qulipta | 10-60mg daily | Preventive |
| Zavegepant | Zavicefta | Nasal spray | Acute |

### Key Advantages

- **Fast onset**: Effect within hours
- **No vasoconstriction**: Safe in cardiovascular disease
- **Preventive + acute**: Rimegepant can do both
- Atogepant works within first week (per 2024 Neurology study)
- No liver toxicity signal (unlike older CGRP agents)

### Expert Consensus (2025)

- First-line for acute if NSAIDs/triptans contraindicated
- Both acute and preventive efficacy
- Good safety profile
