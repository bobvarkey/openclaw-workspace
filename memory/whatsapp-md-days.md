## Key Medical Topics Discussed

### 1. Lipoprotein(a) - Lp(a) in Stroke (May 2024)
- **Significance:** Genetic risk factor for CVD/stroke
- **Levels:** Target <30-50 mg/dL
- **Management:**
  - Lifestyle has limited impact (genetic)
  - Niacin, PCSK9 inhibitors
  - Apheresis for extreme cases
  - Emerging: Pelacarsen antisense oligonucleotides
