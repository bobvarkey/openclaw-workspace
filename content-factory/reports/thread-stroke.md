**2/6**

The KEY: CT perfusion mismatch
- Core infarct vs penumbra imaging
- DAWN/DEFUSE3 criteria still rule

Don't dismiss late presenters. Transfer if able.

**3/6**

New antithrombotics in AF-related stroke:
- DOACs > warfarin now standard
- Apixaban/Rivaroxaban preferred
- Earlier restart (1-7 days) after ICH on DOACs

---

**4/6**

Blood pressure post-acute stroke:
- Permissive hypertension first 24h
- SBP <220 if no ICH
- Cautious lowering protects penumbra

---

**5/6**

Secondary prevention gems:
- LDL <70 post-TIA/stroke (ESPRIT trial)
- Dual antiplatelet? MATCH trial shows Clopidogrel+ASA in first 21 days
- Aspirin still baseline

---

**6/6**

The game-changer: Mobile stroke units
- CT angiography in ambulance
- tPA in the field
- Saves ~20 min to treatment

**Takeaway:** Stroke care is evolving. Time is brain, but now we have MORE time for the right patient.
